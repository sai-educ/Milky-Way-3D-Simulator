# Credits and References

This simulation was improved based on the following scientific references and discussions:

## Data Sources
*   **Structure:** Based on the "two major arms" model (Perseus and Scutum-Centaurus) with a central bar (SBc type).
    *   Reference: [Milky Way - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Milky_Way) (Extracted 12/2/2025)
*   **Solar Position:** Orion Spur (Local Arm), approx. 26,000 light-years from the Galactic Center.
*   **Colors:** Scientific discussion on "true" galaxy colors vs. artistic license.
    *   Reference: [Colors of the Milkyway - discuss.pixls.us](https://discuss.pixls.us/t/colors-of-the-milkyway/18382) (Extracted 12/2/2025)

## Visual Assets
*   **Star Texture:** Procedurally generated within `script.js` to avoid external dependencies.
*   **Font:** Segoe UI / Arial (system fonts).

## Libraries
*   **Three.js:** [threejs.org](https://threejs.org/)
*   **GSAP:** [greensock.com](https://greensock.com/gsap/) (for camera animations)
